ABOUT THE BAND

Hailing from Taufkirchen (Vils), Germany, we are LYMINA, a young five-piece collective crafting a sound as diverse as our influences. With a unique lineup of two singer/guitarists, a synth wizard, a bassist, and a powerhouse drummer, our music is a vibrant journey through the landscapes of indie. We blend the energy of punk, the catchiness of pop, the drive of rock, and the laid-back vibes of reggae into something truly our own. Welcome to our world.
